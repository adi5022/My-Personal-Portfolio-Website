[Projects]

FlowState AI

Python · PyQt6 · scikit-learn · Plotly · Gemini / Groq

Patient-specific cardiovascular digital twin for hypertension progression modelling

  • Simulates blood pressure trajectory, kidney function (eGFR) decline, and survival probability year-by-year over 30 years from 10 clinical inputs — every coefficient traceable to peer-reviewed literature.
  • Designed a two-layer Partial Linear Model for eGFR: explicit clinical equations (age, sex, sodium load) layered with a Gradient Boosting residual — achieving CV R² of 0.957.
  • Built ACE Inhibitor intervention with probabilistic adverse-effect simulation; renders treated vs. untreated divergence via interactive Plotly charts with a year-slider.
  • Integrated ARIA, an LLM chatbot (Gemini / Groq backend), for interpreting simulation results in plain language inside the PyQt6 GUI.
